Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

Total: Approximately 10 minutes

Preparation: 5 minutes

Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs Salt
  • to taste Pepper
  • to taste 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.
  2. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture
  3. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  4. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs
  5. As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
